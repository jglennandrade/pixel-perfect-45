## Interview Data Used for Pricing

### Kevin (Freelance Bookkeeper)
- 90-360 receipts/month per client
- 1-7 clients
- 3-4 hrs/day encoding
- ₱5,000/mo on tools

### Noel (Institution)
- 600-800 receipts/month (30-40/day)
- 7.5 hrs/day encoding
- ₱50-100K/mo on software
- International clients (QuickBooks)

### Cost Per Receipt
- AI cost: ~₱0.10/receipt
- 500 receipts/mo = ₱50 cost
- Margins: 94-99% at all tiers
