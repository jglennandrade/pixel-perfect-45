## Section 1: Pricing

### Structure: 3-Tier Lifetime Deal (Free Beta → Paid Launch)

**Phase 1A (Apr 11-20): Free Beta**
- First 20 bookkeepers get full access free
- Exchange: feedback + permission to use results as proof
- Beta users auto-upgraded to Pro free at launch → become advocates

**Phase 1B (Week 3 Launch): 3-Tier Lifetime**

| Tier | Price | Receipts/mo | Key Features | Target |
|------|-------|-------------|--------------|--------|
| Starter | ₱1,999 | 100 | Single upload, Excel+CSV only, 1 Book | Decoy — intentionally limited |
| **Pro** | **₱4,999** | **500** | **Batch upload, all exports, 5 Books, tags, priority, auto tax** | **Sweet spot — most buyers** |
| Business | ₱9,999 | Unlimited | Everything + future features, integrations*, BIR*, founder access | Firms & institutions |

*\* = roadmap items*

### Pricing Logic
- Kevin's data: spends ₱5K/mo on tools, encodes 3-4 hrs/day. ₱4,999 one-time = 1 month's current spend for lifetime access.
- Noel's data: spends ₱50-100K/mo on systems. ₱9,999 = less than 1 month.
- Hiring an encoder: ₱8-15K/mo. Resibo Pro is cheaper than 1 month of a person.
- AI cost per receipt: ~₱0.10. At 500 receipts/mo = ₱50/mo cost. Margins: 94-99%.
- Starter exists as decoy — 100 receipts/mo is not enough for any real bookkeeper, pushing them to Pro.

### Revenue Projections (10 buyers)
- Conservative (3 Starter + 5 Pro + 2 Biz): ₱50,990
- Monthly infra cost: ~₱2,900. Profitable from Day 1.

### Post-Launch
- Subscriptions open later at ₱1,500-2,000/month (makes lifetime feel like a steal)
- New features go to subscription first, then unlock for lifetime users at their tier

## Section 4: Growth Lever Activation Map

### Lever 1: Nobody Knows You Exist
- **When:** Day 1. Never stops.
- **Channel:** Content (TikTok/Reels) + Warm Outreach (FB Groups + DMs)
- **Actions:** Morning 4 hours = create, post, engage, DM. 1 video/day min. Post in 2-3 groups daily. DM every commenter.
- **Measure:** TikTok views, group joins/day, Messenger list size, DMs sent/day

### Lever 2: Shrink Your Competition
- **When:** Ongoing.
- **Actions:** Never mention competitors by name. Outproduce on content. Own "PH bookkeeper receipt encoding." Kill hate with kindness.
- **Measure:** Are people asking "what's Resibo?" in groups?

### Lever 3: Clear Not Clever
- **When:** Every piece of copy before it goes live.
- **Actions:** Landing page in English at Grade 3-5. Social in Taglish. Kill jargon. Test on non-tech bookkeeper.
- **Measure:** Hemingway grade level. Comment sentiment.

### Lever 4: Proof Over Promise
- **When:** Friday April 11. MVP done → beta starts.
- **Actions:** Send beta access. Collect screenshots, accuracy stats, testimonials. Record demo video. Beta users → Pro free at launch.
- **Measure:** 3+ quotes, 5+ screenshots, 1 demo video, accuracy data by end of Week 2.

### Lever 5: The Hook
- **When:** Every video, post, DM.
- **Actions:** Proof+Promise+Plan formula. Zero dead air. First frame = hook. If video underperforms = hook failed, repost with new hook.
- **Measure:** TikTok view-through rate (first 3 sec).

### Lever 6: More (Volume)
- **When:** Week 2+.
- **Actions:** Scale from 5 to 7-8 videos/week. Post in groups daily. DM everyone. Repurpose 1→5.
- **Measure:** Total pieces/week. DMs/day.

### Lever 7: Word of Mouth (Protect Downside)
- **When:** Always. Critical.
- **Actions:** Free beta = quiet mistakes. Fix fast. Honest launch copy. Reply to complaints in 2 hours. Monitor cost per lead.
- **Measure:** Group comment tone. Complaint volume.

### Lever 8: Steal From Yourself (70/20/10)
- **When:** End of Week 1.
- **Actions:** Sunday KPI review → identify winner → make 3-4 copies with same hook/format. 70% repeat, 20% adjacent, 10% new.
- **Measure:** Content mix ratio.

### Lever 9: High-Info vs Low-Info (70/30 Give-to-Ask)
- **When:** Ongoing.
- **Actions:** Week 1 = all give. Week 2 = give+tease. Week 3 = give+ask. Post-launch: 3-4 value posts per 1 sales post.
- **Schwartz mapping:** Unaware (V1-3) → Problem Aware (V4-5) → Solution Aware (V6-8) → Product Aware (V9-13) → Most Aware (V14-15)
- **Measure:** Content ratio. Audience growth. Unfollows after ask posts.
